Lemon Bars

Makes 10-12 Servings

Shortbread Crust

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1/4 confectionate sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/8 tap salt

Lemon Filling

  • 1 cup granulated white sugar
  • 2 large egges
  • 1/3 lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp flour

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  • Butter or spray with a non stick vegetable spray a 8 inch by 8 inch pan.
  • Beat the butter and sugar until creamy and smooth.
  • Add the flour and salt and beat until the dough starts to come together.
  • Press onto the bottom of your prepped pan and bake for 18-20 minutes.
  • Place on a wire rack to cool while you make the filling.
  • Beat the sugar and eggs until creamy and smooth.
  • Add the lemon zest and store to combine.
  • Fold in flour. Pour the filling over the shortbread crust and bake for 17-20 minutes.
  • Place on wire rack to cool.
  • Cut into squares and dust with powdered sugar.
